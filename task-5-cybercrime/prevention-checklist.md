# Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students



##### General Online Safety

✔ Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
✔ Always remember: you do NOT need a PIN to receive money
✔ Do not click on unknown or suspicious links sent via SMS, WhatsApp, or email
✔ Verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies
✔ Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
✔ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
✔ Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history



##### UPI / Financial Safety Tips

✔ Double-check payment requests before approving them
✔ Use official apps only (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
✔ Immediately decline unknown “collect” requests

##### In Case of Fraud:

✔ Report immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
✔ Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
✔ Inform your bank as soon as possible
